About Me

Just trying to make cool things.

Hey, I'm Greg - a maker of all sorts of things. I'm from the suburbs of New York City (about 45 minutes north of Manhattan), but currently live in Philadelphia studying Entrepreneurship, Marketing, and Interactive Digital Media.

Outside of school I spend my time dabbling with anything that catches my attention - recently anything at the intersection of technology and art catches my attention. Cryptocurrencies, web3, NFTs, website design, photography, etc. are all things of interest at the moment.

Besides personal projects, I work as a freelance website developer and help uncover new opportunities at Beautiful Destinations, a creative agency with one of the largest travel communities on social media with 30M+ followers.

My core passions revolve around tech, creativity, and travel which all tie directly into my work. I have always been interested in creating and building small businesses, and the short-term goal is to build nimble online businesses while living the slow travel lifestyle.
